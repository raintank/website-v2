it's gotten complicated, frustrating and expensive.

It's become a massive hallucination. People get bombarded with confusing alerts, so they can stare at nonsensical charts while half awake, just to see if a problem even exists.

data deluge

Traffic's way up, but so is the amount of metrics, messages and alerts from your ever-growing stack. More data. More software. More alarms.

terrible choices

A proprietary solution? SaaS? It'll be expensive, and you'll lose control. Open source? Could be a time sink.

fractured vision

You want correlation and collaboration across your whole stack. Disparate tools and data make seeing the whole picture hard.

it doesn't have to be so bad.

raintank is fixing it.

an opensaas monitoring engine

We believe all infrastructure platform software is destined to be open source. Our OpenSaaS platform combines the turnkey and scalable nature of a SaaS offering, with all of the advantages of open source software. Run raintank in the cloud, in your datacenter, or as a hybrid deployment. Take back control of your data and destiny, and eliminate lock­-in.

that's gorgeously powerful,

Leveraging Grafana, the leading open source metrics dashboard, we are building a gorgeously powerful frontend that allows you to view, distill and share clear insights about your infrastructure. Grafana2 will plug into raintank to provide a beautiful and seamless experience that democratizes monitoring. Who said open source wasn't sexy?

and unifies data and understanding.

Already have Graphite? OpenTSDB? InfluxDB? Connect it to the platform, whether that data is on-premise or in the cloud. Add tons of capabilities around these existing databases, and get a unified view of what's going on. Users, networks, applications, instances, devices and zones: we look at all horizons of your stack. This broad view allows for unprecedented context and situational awareness.
