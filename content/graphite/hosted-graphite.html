We're excited to open up a beta version of our new
hosted metrics platform.

Our beta hosted Graphite is powered by metrictank, a 100% open source Graphite-compatible TSDB we're developing and recently wrote about.

Metrictank, along with Cassandra and its other dependencies can be considered an alternative to the carbon and whisper components of a traditional Graphite implementation. Early tests have shown a 10x reduction in disk space and a similar decrease on Cassandra load.

High Performance

We care about efficiency, throughput and compresson. You care about accuracy and reliability.

Extremely Scalable

Our hosted Graphite is completely scalable. It can grow as your number of metrics grows.

Easy to Get Started

Forget about the complicated installation and configuration of setting up Graphite on-prem.

100% Open Source

We want to change the rules of “SaaS vs. open source” monitoring. It's about choices, not lock-in.
