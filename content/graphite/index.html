We are investing heavily in the Graphite open source project, and are committed to its continued success.

Graphite is an enterprise-ready monitoring tool that runs equally well on cheap hardware or Cloud infrastructure. Teams use Graphite to track the performance of their websites, applications, business services, and networked servers. In short, Graphite makes it easy to store and graph metrics.
