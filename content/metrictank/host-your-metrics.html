Features

High Performance

Scale to many millions of series, ingest large amounts of data, and enjoy lightning quick responses. All while using a fraction of the storage.

100% Graphite Compatible

We love Graphite, and its extensive query language. metrictank is fully compatible and works seamlessly with all your existing tools and dashboards.

Built with Proven Tech

Need clustering that “just works”? metrictank uses the proven, rock-solid Cassandra for highly reliable clustered storage that works at massive scale.

Metrics 2.0

We created metrics 2.0, and metrictank aims to offer all of its advantages. This will include tags in Graphite, automatic units in your Dashboards, and many other awesome improvements!

OpenSaaS

metrictank is completely open source, but also available as hosted offering, so that you can leave the heavy lifting to us. We believe in choices, not lock-in.
